# 10 Financing Enablers for Industrial Decarbonization

February 2026 UNIDO report identifies ten financing enablers to scale industrial decarbonization from pilots to investment pipelines, using cement and concrete as primary reference. Enablers span the full investment chain: transition planning, standards, and disclosure frameworks; fiscal incentives, blended finance, guarantees, and demand-side instruments; plus prudential, supervisory, and market-infrastructure measures.

These address barriers like high capital costs and technology risk, complementing existing policy and technology resources in the repository.

[Source: UNIDO Industrial Decarbonization](https://decarbonization.unido.org/resources/10-financing-enablers-industrial-decarbonization/)
