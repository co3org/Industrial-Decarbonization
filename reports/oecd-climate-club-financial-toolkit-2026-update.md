# OECD Climate Club Financial Toolkit 2026 Update

The OECD/Climate Club released the 2026 edition of the Climate Club Financial Toolkit on 26 May 2026. It provides a comprehensive overview of 28 economic, de-risking, and financing instruments for industrial decarbonisation, with case studies applicable to both emerging markets and developing economies (EMDEs) and developed countries.

Key sections cover:
- Economic instruments (e.g., carbon pricing, subsidies)
- De-risking tools (e.g., guarantees, insurance)
- Financing mechanisms tailored to hard-to-abate sectors

This builds on the 2025 toolkit and aligns with ongoing work in the repo's financing and policy sections.

Source: [OECD Climate Club Financial Toolkit 2026 Update](https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/climate-club-financial-toolkit-2026-update_df71ab89-en.html) [https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/financing-solutions-to-foster-industrial-decarbonisation-in-emerging-and-developing-economies_24a155ab-en.html]
