# OECD Climate Club Financial Toolkit 2026 Update

The OECD released its Climate Club Financial Toolkit 2026 Update in May 2026, providing economic, de-risking, and financing instruments specifically tailored for industry decarbonisation. [https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/climate-club-financial-toolkit-2026-update_df71ab89-en.html](https://www.oecd.org/en/publications/climate-club-financial-toolkit-2026-update_df71ab89-en.html)

## Key Instruments
- De-risking mechanisms for low-carbon technology investments in hard-to-abate sectors.
- Blended finance models combining public and private capital.
- Policy frameworks to enhance competitiveness while advancing decarbonisation goals.

This update builds on prior Climate Club efforts and aligns with global initiatives like those from UNIDO and national programs covered elsewhere in the repository.
