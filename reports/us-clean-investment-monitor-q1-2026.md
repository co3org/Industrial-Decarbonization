# US Clean Investment Monitor: Q1 2026 Update on Industrial Decarbonization

In Q1 2026, $25 billion was invested in clean energy production and industrial decarbonization, a 6% decline from Q4 2025 but a 15% increase compared to Q1 2025. [Clean Investment Monitor](https://www.cleaninvestmentmonitor.org/reports/us-q1-2026-update)

Industrial decarbonization investment totaled roughly $1 billion in Q1 2026, jumping by over a fourth from the previous quarter, but down 8% relative to Q1 2025. New announced investment in clean energy and industry decarbonization totaled $62 billion in Q1 2026, with a significant portion (43%) tied to a $27 billion nuclear project. [Clean Investment Monitor](https://www.cleaninvestmentmonitor.org/reports/us-q1-2026-update)

This update highlights shifting investment patterns in industrial decarbonization amid broader clean energy trends, providing key benchmarks for tracking progress toward net-zero goals in heavy industry.
