# DOE Loan Programs Office Support for Industrial Decarbonization Projects

The U.S. Department of Energy's Loan Programs Office (LPO) plays a critical role in financing large-scale industrial decarbonization initiatives. Through programs accelerated by the Bipartisan Infrastructure Law and Inflation Reduction Act, LPO supports projects that reduce greenhouse gas emissions in energy-intensive sectors.

Key focus areas include:
- Carbon capture, utilization, and storage (CCUS) deployments
- Electrification and process efficiency upgrades
- Low-carbon fuels and hydrogen infrastructure

Recent LPO investments help lay the foundation for a decarbonized economy, targeting net-zero goals by 2050 with estimated needs of $700 billion to $1 trillion in public-private funding. [Source](https://www.energy.gov/edf/industrial-decarbonization-projects)
