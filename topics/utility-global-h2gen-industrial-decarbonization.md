# Utility Global H2Gen Electrochemical Hydrogen for Industrial Decarbonization (2026)

Utility Global's H2Gen® technology produces cost-effective clean hydrogen and highly concentrated CO₂ streams by leveraging residual electrochemical energy in industrial off-gases and biogenic gases to drive water electrolysis without external electricity input. This enables economic decarbonization for existing assets in hard-to-abate sectors such as steel, cement, chemicals, and refining.

## Key Features and Advantages
- Converts water to pure hydrogen using off-gas energy.
- Concentrates CO₂ as a separate product stream for capture or utilization.
- Low capital and operating costs compared to traditional electrolysis.
- Scalable from small biogas plants to mega-scale industrial facilities.

## Recent Developments
In February 2026, Utility Global announced a $100 million first close of its Series D financing round, led by Ara Partners and APG Asset Management. The funding accelerates global commercial deployment of H2Gen®.

The company has active projects including Industrial H2Gen® deployments planned for 2026-2028, building on earlier pilots like Project Frontier.

## Relevance to Industrial Decarbonization
H2Gen® offers a practical pathway for on-site, low-cost clean hydrogen production integrated with existing industrial processes, complementing green hydrogen and CCUS approaches covered elsewhere in this repository.
