# US Climate Alliance 2026 Industrial Decarbonization Policy Guidebook Addendum

The US Climate Alliance released an updated Policy Guidebook Addendum in February 2026, capturing 60 additional state policies for industrial decarbonization across planning, carbon pricing, incentives, standards, and supporting actions. It covers sectors including chemicals, refining, iron & steel, cement, glass, forest products, and food & beverage, with emphasis on energy efficiency, low-carbon feedstocks, CCUS, electrification, and emerging technologies.

Key elements include updated dashboards and sector-specific pathways aligned with 2026 state progress. [Source](https://usclimatealliance.org/guide/industrial-decarbonization-guide-dec-2022/)

This complements existing US state and federal policy coverage in the repository.
