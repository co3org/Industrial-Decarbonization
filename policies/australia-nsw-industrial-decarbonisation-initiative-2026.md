# NSW Industrial Decarbonisation Initiative (2026)

The New South Wales (NSW) government launched the Industrial Decarbonisation Initiative to co-fund emissions reduction projects across manufacturing and mining sectors. The program provides up to $40 million for the Low Emissions Industry Program, supporting projects that reduce emissions at facilities emitting up to 100,000 tCO2-e per year, with grants between $500,000 and $10 million covering up to 50% of costs. Applications close June 9, 2026.

A parallel Renewable Gas Production Program allocates another $40 million for biomethane facilities. [Source](https://www.energy.nsw.gov.au/business-and-industry/programs-grants-and-schemes/industrial-decarbonisation-initiative)

This complements broader Australian efforts and fills a gap in regional policy coverage for industrial decarbonization funding mechanisms outside North America, EU, and Asia.
