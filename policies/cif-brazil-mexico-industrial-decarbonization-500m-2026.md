# CIF $500M Brazil-Mexico Industrial Decarbonization Programme (2026)

The Climate Investment Funds (CIF) approved a $500 million concessional finance package in June 2026 for industrial decarbonization programmes in Brazil and Mexico. These are the first countries to secure funding under CIF's new Industry Decarbonisation Programme, supporting the shift of high-emitting industries to sustainable practices and unlocking investments in net-zero, climate-resilient business models and technologies. [https://carbon-pulse.com/525650/]

## Key Details
- Focus: Heavy industry decarbonization in developing countries, including pilot and scale-up of new technologies.
- Eligible sectors: Energy-intensive industries aligned with CIF's broader Industry Decarbonization Program.
- Impact: Helps ensure developing countries are not left behind in global industrial decarbonization efforts.
