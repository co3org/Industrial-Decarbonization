# China Three-Year Action Plan for Energy-Saving and Carbon-Reduction Retrofits in Key Industries (2026-2028)

China's National Development and Reform Commission (NDRC) issued a new three-year action plan in June 2026 targeting intensive energy conservation and carbon reduction upgrades across nine heavy industries from 2026 to 2028. [https://www.carbonbrief.org/china-briefing-25-june-2026-five-year-plans-passed-critical-mineral-tensions-industrial-decarbonisation-plan/]

## Key Industries Covered
- Steel
- Electrolytic aluminum
- Cement
- Flat glass
- Oil refining
- Ethylene
- Synthetic ammonia
- Methanol
- Coal-fired power

## Objectives
The plan aims to enhance enterprises' energy and carbon efficiency, significantly advancing green and low-carbon development in these sectors through retrofits and upgrades. [https://www.carbonbrief.org/china-briefing-25-june-2026-five-year-plans-passed-critical-mineral-tensions-industrial-decarbonisation-plan/]

This complements China's broader 15th Five-Year Plan (2026-2030) efforts on industrial decarbonization.
