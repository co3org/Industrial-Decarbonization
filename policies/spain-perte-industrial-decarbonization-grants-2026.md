# Spain PERTE Industrial Decarbonization Grants €518M to 17 Projects (June 2026)

Spain's Ministry of Industry and Tourism awarded €518 million in recovery grants under the PERTE (Strategic Projects for Economic Recovery and Transformation) for Industrial Decarbonization to 17 projects focused on decarbonizing industrial processes. Cement emerged as the top beneficiary sector. [https://www.spglobal.com/energy/en/news-research/latest-news/natural-gas/062526-spain-grants-eur518-mil-to-industrial-decarbonization-projects-cement-top-gainer](https://www.spglobal.com/energy/en/news-research/latest-news/natural-gas/062526-spain-grants-eur518-mil-to-industrial-decarbonization-projects-cement-top-gainer)

This funding is part of Spain's broader €3.2 billion public investment commitment under the PERTE framework, expected to mobilize up to €11.8 billion in total investment. The initiative supports process decarbonization in hard-to-abate sectors through grants for efficiency improvements, fuel switching, and emissions reduction technologies.

## Key Details
- **Award Date**: June 2026
- **Total Grant Amount**: €518 million
- **Number of Projects**: 17
- **Focus**: Industrial process decarbonization, with cement sector leading allocations
- **Context**: Part of Spain's Recovery, Transformation and Resilience Plan (RRP) aligned with EU decarbonization goals
