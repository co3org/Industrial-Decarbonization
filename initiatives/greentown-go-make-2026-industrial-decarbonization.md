# Greentown Go Make 2026 Open-Innovation Program for Industrial Decarbonization

Greentown Labs, the world's largest climatetech and energy incubator, launched the Go Make 2026 open-innovation program in partnership with Shell Catalysts & Technologies and Technip Energies. The program focuses on catalytic solutions for industrial decarbonization and the energy transition, targeting startups with technology readiness levels of 3-8 developing process technology and catalytic innovations for low-carbon fuels, low-carbon gases, CO₂ removal, and alternative chemical manufacturing.

The program held a special event in Houston, Texas, on June 9, 2026, where Technip Energies selected top innovators from 148 original candidates. Five startups will participate in the cohort.

This initiative accelerates practical solutions for hard-to-abate industrial sectors through open innovation and collaboration between startups, corporates, and incubators.
